FIGURE



HEADS YOU PROMENADE THREE QUARTERS ROUND THE RING
HEADS/SIDE TWO COUPLES RIGHT AND LEFT THRU, TURN THE GIRL I SING
YOU PASS THRU, SWING THRU AND THEN THE BOYS RUN RIGHT 
YOU BEND THE LINE MOVE UP AND BACK, TOUCH A QUARTER TONIGHT
COLUMN CIRCULATE, AND THEN THE BOYS RUN RIGHT

SWING THAT CORNER GIRL AND PROMENADE TO NIGHT
