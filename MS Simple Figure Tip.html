=== SQT4 Ferris Wheel ===

Heads/Side – Promenade Half-way.

Right and Left Thru

Ferris Wheel and go

=== Stars ===

Heads Square Thru count 'em 4 hands round

Right Hand Star turn it once around

Back by the Left, roll it one time and then

back to your corner and do a Dosado

Swing Thru and then the Boys Trade

AL
